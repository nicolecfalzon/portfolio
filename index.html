About me

The through-line is always impact

I've spent 10+ years at the intersection of operations, product, and growth — at companies that were moving fast and needed someone who could build the systems to keep up. What I've learned is that the best product work doesn't happen despite the operational foundation — it happens because of it.

At ClassPass, I was deep in the weeds with 5,000+ small business partners. At Pinata, I owned strategy and execution through a full business model transition. At The Unquantifiable, I ran parallel delivery across 7 client teams as a fractional PM. The common thread: I show up where the work is hard and stay until it's done right.

I work best with teams that value ownership, move quickly, and care about building things that actually serve the end user — not just check the box.
